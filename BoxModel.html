Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower, located in Paris, France, is one of the most iconic structures in the world. Built in 1889 for the World's Fair, it stands 324 meters tall and attracts millions of visitors each year.

Pyramid Of Giza

The Pyramids of Giza in Egypt are ancient monuments that date back over 4,500 years. They were constructed as tombs for the pharaohs and are the last surviving wonder of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.

Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty is a symbol of freedom and democracy, located in New York Harbor, USA. A gift from France in 1886, it stands 93 meters tall and welcomes visitors arriving by sea.
